Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I use the Kibo Paste keyboard extension?

To enable the keyboard extension, follow these steps:

  1. Go to iOS Settings > General > Keyboard > Keyboards
  2. Tap "Add New Keyboard" and select "Kibo Paste"
  3. Select "Kibo Paste" and enable "Allow Full Access"
  4. Open any app that supports keyboard input and tap on a text field
  5. Switch to the Kibo Paste keyboard (tap the globe icon or press and hold the keyboard switch button)

2. Why does the app need "Full Access" permission?

Kibo Paste requires "Full Access" permission to read and write your saved paste content. Rest assured, we do not collect your personal data or keyboard inputs - this permission is solely used to access your paste data.

3. Where is my paste data stored?

Your paste data is securely stored in your device's local storage. Each paste is saved on your device until you choose to delete it.

4. The app is not working properly. What should I do?

Please try the following steps:

  1. Make sure your iOS is updated to the latest version
  2. Restart the app
  3. Restart your device
  4. If the issue persists, please contact us using the information below

5. Is my paste data secure?

Yes, your paste data is securely stored on your device. We do not collect or store any of your paste data, and all information remains on your device, ensuring your data privacy and security.
