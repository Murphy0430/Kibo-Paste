Privacy Policy for Kibo Paste

Last Updated: June 5, 2024

Introduction

Kibo Paste ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our Kibo Paste application ("App").

Kibo Paste is a productivity application that allows users to create, store, and access notes and text snippets via the main app interface and a custom keyboard extension.

NO DATA COLLECTION

We do not collect any personal data from users of Kibo Paste.

The Kibo Paste application:

  • Does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • Does not track your usage or behavior
  • Does not include any analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not share any data with third parties
  • Does not use your data for advertising purposes
  • Does not access your internet connection for data collection

Local Data Storage

All notes and text snippets that you create within Kibo Paste are stored locally on your device. If you choose to enable iCloud synchronization (which is optional), your notes will be stored in your personal iCloud account, which is managed by Apple in accordance with their privacy policy.

Keyboard Access

The Kibo Paste keyboard extension requires "Full Access" permission to function properly. This permission is needed to:

  • Access your saved notes from the main app
  • Allow copy and paste functionality between the keyboard and other apps

Even with Full Access enabled, we do not collect, transmit, or store any information about what you type or which applications you use.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
